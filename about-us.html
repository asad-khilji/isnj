About ISNJ


The Islamic Society of North Jersey (ISNJ) was established in February 1999 in Budd Lake, NJ upon the guiding principles of the Qur’an and Sunnah. The community began with 75+ members and now contains 200+ active members, Alhamdulillah.


We aim to provide Muslims within the northern New Jersey area a place where they can:


Perform their Islamic acts of worship, including five daily congregational prayers


Connect with other Muslim families


Send their children (elementary to high school) to Sunday School and follow a formal curriculum in Arabic, Quran & Islamic Studies


Attend Friday Prayer (Jummah Khutba)


Attend Eid Prayer and participate in festivities


Join in community dinners and family halaqas


ISNJ is an active member of Mount Olive Clergy Association – an interfaith organization that fosters healthy relationships across different groups of faiths. We also have a healthy relationship with Mount Olive city officials.
